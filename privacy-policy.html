Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Life in UK Test Tracker Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles user data.

Data Collection

The Extension collects the following data from the Life in the UK Test practice website (lifeintheuktestweb.co.uk):

  • Quiz questions and answer choices
  • Your selected answers
  • Correct answers
  • Whether your answers were correct or incorrect
  • Timestamps of when you completed questions

The Extension does not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or account details.

Data Storage

All collected data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). Your data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Display your quiz statistics and performance metrics
  • Allow you to review past practice sessions
  • Enable export of your data in JSON or CSV format for personal analysis

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell or transfer user data to third parties
  • Use data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Share data with any external services or servers
  • Use data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains stored locally until you choose to delete it. You can clear all stored data at any time using the "Clear All Data" button in the Extension's side panel. Uninstalling the Extension will also remove all stored data.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email provided on the Chrome Web Store listing.
